Tadrar-Grarem Gouga Sports and Leisure Park

This work focuses on the design of a sustainable sports park in the city of Grarem Gouga, Mila. The project aims to address the challenge of promoting physical activity and user well-being while preserving the natural environment and integrating the principles of neuroarchitecture. The study led to the selection of a suitable site for the project (the Tadrar Forest) and the identification of its main components: sports, recreation, recovery, and accommodation. The final project, titled “Tadrar-Grarem Gouga Sports and Leisure Park,” was designed with careful consideration of the material and contextual aspects of the city of Grarem Gouga.Revit was used for the design, Enscape for visualization, and Photoshop for creating the master plan.
